## General Storage Patterns
1. **Drawers**: Small items, utensils, personal items
2. **Shelves**: General storage, containers, supplies
3. **Countertops**: Frequently used items, in-progress tasks
4. **Carts**: Mobile storage, cleaning supplies, transport items
5. **Holders**: Designated for specific items (towels, paper)

## ALFWorld Specific Notes
- Objects often have numbers (e.g., "cart 1", "drawer 2")
- Receptacle names are concatenated (e.g., "bathtubbasin", "sinkbasin")
- Observations list receptacles before objects on them
- "On the [receptacle], you see..." indicates visible objects
