# Common Device Locations in ALFWorld

This guide lists typical receptacles where specific devices are found. Use this to prioritize search order in **Phase 1** of the skill.

## Lighting & Electronics
*   **Desklamp:** `desk`, `sidetable`, `dresser`
*   **Floorlamp:** Next to `armchair`, `sofa`, in `corner`
*   **Cellphone:** `sidetable`, `desk`, `bed`

## Kitchen Appliances
*   **Microwave:** `countertop`, `kitchen cabinet`
*   **Fridge:** Standalone unit (often its own receptacle like `fridge 1`)
*   **Faucet:** `sink`, `bathtub`
*   **Toaster:** `countertop`
*   **Coffee Machine:** `countertop`

## Cleaning Tools
*   **Spraybottle:** `countertop`, `sink`, `cabinet`
*   **Soapbar:** `sink`, `soapdish`, `shower`

## Climate Control
*   **Houseplant:** `floor`, `sidetable`, `desk`
*   **Candle:** `sidetable`, `mantel`, `desk`

## General Search Heuristics
1.  **Check the most specific receptacle first** (e.g., for a `desklamp`, check `desk 1` before `sidetable 1`).
2.  **If not found, check similar receptacles** in the room.
3.  **Devices are often static** and do not move between receptacles during a task.
