## Conference-Specific Patterns
### CVPR Paper Titles Often Include:
- Technical method names (e.g., "VideoDPO", "SANA-Video", "MotionCtrl")
- "Towards..." for exploratory work
- "A Survey of..." for review papers
- "[Method]: [Description]" format
- Acronyms followed by explanations

### NeurIPS/ICML Patterns:
- More theoretical/methodological focus
- "Learning", "Optimization", "Theory" in titles
- Mathematical formulations
- Algorithm development

## Filtering Strategies
### Positive Signals (Include):
- Papers with generative/diffusion/keywords in title
- Authors with multiple papers in the domain
- Clear professor titles (Prof., Associate Prof., Assistant Prof.)
- Established research labs/groups

### Negative Signals (Exclude or Verify):
- Student authors (Ph.D. candidates)
- Industry researchers (unless specified)
- Unrelated research areas (robotics, medical imaging without generative focus)
- One-off papers in the domain
