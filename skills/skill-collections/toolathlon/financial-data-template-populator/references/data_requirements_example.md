# Example Data Requirements (data.txt)
This file specifies the data to be fetched and populated into specific Excel sheets.

## Sheet 1: Basic Trend
*   Historical end-of-quarter stock prices, outstanding shares, and market cap for each quarter from [Start Quarter] to [End Quarter].
*   Example: "Historical end-of-quarter NVDA stock prices, outstanding shares, and market cap for each quarter from 2024 Q3 to 2025 Q2"

## Sheet 2: Major Holders Summary
*   Summary of major holders: insiders held percentage, institutions held percentage, and number of institutions.
*   Example: "Summary of major holders: insiders held percentage, institutions held percentage, and number of institutions"

## Sheet 3: Key Shareholders Details
*   Details on the top 10 institutional shareholders: shareholder name, shares held, holding value, holding ratio.
*   Example: "Details on the top 10 institutional shareholders: shareholder name, shares held, holding value, holding ratio"
