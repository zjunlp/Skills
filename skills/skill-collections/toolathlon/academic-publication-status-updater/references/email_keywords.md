# Email Keywords for Paper Acceptance

Use these keywords to search and identify acceptance emails.

## Primary Search Queries
*   "accepted"
*   "camera-ready"
*   "congratulations"
*   "notification"
*   "decision"

## Conference & Venue Identifiers
*   COML (Conference on Machine Learning)
*   COAI (Conference on Artificial Intelligence)
*   COLM (Conference on Language Modeling)
*   ICML (International Conference on Machine Learning)
*   NeurIPS (Neural Information Processing Systems)
*   ICLR (International Conference on Learning Representations)
*   ACL (Association for Computational Linguistics)
*   EMNLP (Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing)
*   CVPR (Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition)
*   ICCV (International Conference on Computer Vision)
*   ECCV (European Conference on Computer Vision)
*   AAAI (Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence)
*   IJCAI (International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence)
*   Workshop / Workshop on

## Key Phrases in Email Body
*   "We are pleased to inform you"
*   "has been accepted"
*   "Your submission was successful"
*   "top % of submissions"
*   "oral presentation"
*   "poster presentation"
*   "Please prepare your camera-ready version"
*   "deadline for the final version"
