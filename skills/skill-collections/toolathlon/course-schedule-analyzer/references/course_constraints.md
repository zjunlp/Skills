# Common Course Constraints

## Course Categories
- **思政B**: Ideological and Political Theory (B category)
- **通识教育核心课程**: General Education Core Curriculum
- **专业课程**: Major courses
- **公共艺术课程**: Public art courses

## Assessment Methods
- **开卷**: Open-book exam
- **闭卷**: Closed-book exam
- **论文**: Essay/Paper
- **其他**: Other (e.g., report, presentation)

## Days of Week (Chinese)
- **周一**: Monday
- **周二**: Tuesday
- **周三**: Wednesday
- **周四**: Thursday
- **周五**: Friday
- **周六**: Saturday
- **周日**: Sunday

## Campus Locations
- **邯郸校区**: Handan Campus
- **江湾校区**: Jiangwan Campus
- **枫林校区**: Fenglin Campus
- **张江校区**: Zhangjiang Campus

## Time Conflict Checking
- Courses on the same day with overlapping periods conflict.
- Example: "周一 第2-3节" conflicts with "周一 第3-4节".
- Example: "周一 第2-3节" does NOT conflict with "周一 第4-5节".
