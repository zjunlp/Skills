# Common Invoice Formats & Extraction Patterns

## 1. Software/Service Invoices (e.g., Excalidraw, Overleaf)
**Key Sections:**
- **Vendor**: Company name at top or in footer
- **Date**: "Date of issue", "Billed On", "Transaction date"
- **Amount**: "Total", "Amount due" followed by `$XX.XX USD`
- **Tax**: Often included in the total amount
